# Getting started

## Create an account

Open the RunSpace registration page and follow the instructions shown on
the screen.

Depending on the selected account method, you may need to save an account
key or verify your email address.

Keep your account key somewhere private and secure. Do not send it to
another person.

## Sign in

RunSpace supports the available sign-in methods shown by your client,
including username and password or account-key login.

You may also be asked to complete two-factor authentication or a human
verification check.

## Set up your profile

After signing in, you can configure available profile details such as:

- Profile picture
- Banner
- Biography
- Status
- Privacy settings

Avoid placing private contact information in your public profile.

## Add friends

Friend requests can be sent through the available friend interface.

After another user accepts your request, you can start a private
conversation or invite them to supported group features.
