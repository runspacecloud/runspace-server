Quick help
Use the "Forgot password" link on the login page. If you use an Account Key, generate a new one from your registered email.
If you have backup codes, use one to log in. Otherwise open a ticket with category "Account · login, password, 2FA" and we'll verify your identity.
After a key reset you need to log out and back in on all devices. Old messages cannot be decrypted — this is by design for security.
