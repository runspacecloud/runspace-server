# Privacy and safety

## Share carefully

Do not publish or send information such as:

- Passwords or account keys
- Home address
- Financial information
- Identity documents
- Private recovery information
- Content you do not have permission to share

## Media files

Review images, videos and documents before uploading them.

A file may contain information you did not intend to share, including
visible names, locations or document details.

## Blocking

Block users who repeatedly contact you against your wishes or make you feel
unsafe.

Blocking and reporting are separate actions. Submit a report when the
behavior should also be reviewed by RunSpace moderation.

## Community rules

Users must follow the RunSpace community rules.

Content involving threats, exploitation, illegal material, harassment or
abuse may be removed and accounts may be restricted.

## Privacy information

Read the current RunSpace privacy policy and terms on the official
RunSpace website for the complete legal information.
