# Temporary accounts and chats

## Temporary accounts

A temporary account is designed for short-lived access.

Temporary accounts expire automatically after the selected duration and
may have fewer features than a permanent account.

Do not use a temporary account for information you need to keep
permanently.

## Temporary chats

Temporary chats are rooms that expire automatically.

A room may include:

- An invitation code
- A maximum member limit
- An owner
- A visible expiration time

Messages and access may no longer be available after the room expires.

## Invitation codes

Treat invitation codes as private when the conversation is intended for a
limited group.

Anyone who receives a valid invitation may be able to request or gain
access, depending on the room settings.

## Important limitations

Temporary does not mean consequence-free.

Community rules and safety restrictions still apply to temporary accounts
and temporary chats.
