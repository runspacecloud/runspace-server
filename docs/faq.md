# Frequently asked questions

## Why can I not sign in?

Check that you are using the correct sign-in method and that your username,
password or account key was entered correctly.

Complete two-factor authentication or human verification when requested.

## Why did my temporary account disappear?

Temporary accounts expire automatically after their selected lifetime.

## Why did a temporary chat disappear?

Temporary chats are removed or become unavailable when their expiration
time is reached.

## Why does my voice call not connect?

Check microphone permission, your selected audio device, VPN settings and
network connection.

Some networks prevent direct voice connections.

## Why can I not send a large file?

RunSpace applies file-size and file-type restrictions for reliability and
safety.

## Can I recover a lost account key?

Recovery depends on the account type and recovery options configured
before access was lost.

Contact support, but never send your complete account key in an ordinary
support message.

## Is every feature available on every device?

Not necessarily. The web, desktop and Android clients may receive features
at different times.
